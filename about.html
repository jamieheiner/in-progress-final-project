About Me

Just a little bit.

A bright and determined student at Brigham Young University, Jamie is working tirelessly toward her goal of being accepted into the highly competitive BYU Marriott School of Business. As a sophomore majoring in pre-business, she has dedicated herself to excelling academically while actively exploring opportunities to develop her leadership and analytical skills. Whether it's spending late nights in the library studying for exams or engaging in group projects, Jamie's commitment to learning and self-improvement shines through in every aspect of her college experience.

In her second year, Jamie has focused on building a strong foundation for her future goals by seeking mentorship from upperclassmen who have successfully navigated the application process. She frequently attends workshops and career panels hosted by the business school to better understand the expectations and opportunities within the program. Additionally, she has taken on part-time work in an on-campus office, where she's gaining practical experience in organization, communication, and teamwork. These experiences have helped her refine her career aspirations and build a support network to guide her along the way.
